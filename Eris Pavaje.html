About me

Welcome to my 3D design portfolio! My name is Eris Pavaje and I am a 3D artist specializing in creating high-quality game assets and architectural renders. With 2 years of experience in the industry, I have developed a strong set of skills in 3D modeling, texturing, and lighting, and I also have a passion for creating immersive and realistic environments. In my career, I have had the opportunity to work with a variety of clients on a range of projects, including creating assets for various indie companies game and producing photorealistic renders for local architectural designs.
In my free time I also like to do "random artworks" as I call them, in process that helps me evolve as an artist and freelancer and makes me more content with my work.

The software of my choice is blender but I am always seeking new challenges and opportunities to grow as a 3D artist, and I am excited to share my work with you.

Services

Architecural Renderings

I can provide photorealistic renders, 360 photos and presentation videos for various types of architectural projects, be it indoor or outdoor.

Game-ready assets

I do game assets such as weapons, armors, stage props and any other kind of items based on the client's need. The assets are optimized for performance, with baked textures and a low poly-count if needed.

3D Environments

I do concepts and full scale environments that can be game-ready, production-ready, together with lightning and realistic textures
